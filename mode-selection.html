The classic way of playing Little Llama. Little Llama jumps on floating, moving platforms to accomplish his goal of reaching the coins. The platforms offer cover from the bouncing pigs. Pigs do not bounce off of each other.

No platforms and no jumping. More like flying and gliding. This mode is a little harder because there is nothing blocking the pigs. The pigs bounce off of each other with realistic elastic collision mechanics.
